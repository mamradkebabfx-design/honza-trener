## Personal Story (Key Trust Element)

I have been active in sports my entire life and have always had a strong relationship with movement.  
At a young age, I was diagnosed with ankylosing spondylitis, which led me to develop a deeper interest in fitness and how the human body functions.  

This experience pushed me to educate myself extensively, and everything I teach today has first been tested on myself.  

I focus on safe, effective, and sustainable training that helps improve strength, fitness, and overall well-being.
